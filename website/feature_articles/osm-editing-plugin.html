OSM Editing plugin allows you to make contributions to OpenStreetMap, a global community aimed at creating a comprehensive map of the world and providing up-to date open-source data to every user.

Just launch OsmAnd Maps & Navigation app and update the information about different locations.

How to use it (for Android)

The plugin lets you create new objects, so-called points of interest or POI on the map. New shop opened next to you? Add it to the map! Your favorite monument is missing on the map? Put it there in seconds. Also, if you're the owner of a newly-opened business, adding it to the map is a great way to let people find you.

So how to do it? Go to app menu-> Plugins-> OpenStreetMap editing and press Enable.

Tap on the map where the new POI has to be placed. Press on 'Actions', then choose 'Create POI', add its name and other details like working hours, website, etc. You'll also have to register at openstreetmap.com and then provide your OSM credentials to introduce changes.

Note: you can see your new POI on OsmAnd map after one hour after added it to OSM project if you have OsmAnd Live subscription.

Add tracks

Choose the required track and click Yes. You can change description, tags or visibility of the track. Kinfly note, that OSM editing plugin has to be turned on. In an hour, your track will be added to https://www.openstreetmap.org/traces, so you can view it and other users can find it helpful, as well. You can participate in the work on the global map. Just share the tracks you've made. They become part of the OpenStreetMap project. Make sure you tag objects and roads as well.

Noticed a mistake on the map?

You can also report about the mistakes on the map. All you have to do is tap on the location, choose 'Actions'-> 'Open OSM Note' and then add the info about it. OSM editors would consider your comments.

To view the OSM editing layer, go to app menu-> Configure-> OSM notes (online). You'll see all the notes in the specific area. You can click to OSM note and choose your comment or delete it.

How to use it (for iOS)

First step, you can switch on the plugin: go to app menu-> Maps & Resources-> Plugins-> OpenStreetMap Editing.

This feature lets you create new objects, so-called points of interest or POI on the map and OSM notes.

So how to do it? Tap on the map where the new POI has to be placed 'Actions'->'Create POI' add its name and other details like working hours, website, etc. You'll also have to register at OpenStreetMap and then provide your OSM credentials to introduce changes.

Note: you can see your new POI on OsmAnd map in an hour after it is added to OSM project if you have enabled hourly map updates in OsmAnd Live subscription.

There are all your not upload "Edits" and "Notes" at general menu-> 'My Places'-> 'OSM Edits'. From this menu, you can delete or upload all your edits and notes to OSM project.
